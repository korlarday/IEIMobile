FAQs What is the Contributory Pension Scheme (CPS)?

The Contributory Pension Scheme (CPS) which commenced in 2004 is fully funded, privately managed, with third party in custody of the funds and assests and is based on individual accounts. It ensures that everyone who has worked receives his/her retirement benefits as at when due.
